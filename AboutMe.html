ABOUT ME



HELLO!

My name is Ethan Karpinski.


I've been an artist my whole life. My grandma taught me when I was young, and I have developed my skills through the use of online videos and tutorials, along with a great amount of practice. I'm a sophomore at Michigan State University and I attended highschool in a small town just north of East Lansing. Formerly, I mostly painted and drew with colored pencils and markers, creating a few pictures to hang around the house or display on the fridge. During my Freshman year of college, however, I purchased an iPad for help managing my coursework.

Little did I know,

I was actually going to be flooded with art requests after experimenting on a drawing app and showing some friends. I created an Instagram page and started doing some minor advertisements to gain attention. I reached almost 100 followers on my instagram page and have done paid work for 25+ people. Click the link labeled "EthanDZNS TIKTOK" on the right side of your screen for an example of how I do my work. I'm not dedicating this as a full time job, but it is a hobby that I enjoy and can make a little bit of money on the side. My FAVORITE part of doing this is seeing someone's reaction after giving them their finished artwork.
